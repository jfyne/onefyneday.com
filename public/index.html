Travel & Accomodation

We are getting married in a remote part of Iceland so we are reserving a bus that you can book a seat on to avoid having to rent a car. Icelandic road conditions vary greatly so we highly recommend you book a seat on the bus. The bus will take you on a fun tour with lunch provided and bring you to our wedding venue Arnarstapi Center.

Please reach out to our wedding planner extraordinaire, Hannes Pálsson, by email hannes@pinkiceland.is in order to book your room and reserve a spot on the wedding bus. There are several types of rooms at the venue and Hannes will help you pick the right one.

Note, booking your spot on the bus also gives you a ride back to the airport on Sunday, or to Reykjavík so you can continue your Iceland trip. Hannes has graciously offered his travel planning services to any guests who would like to visit Iceland early or extend their stay after the wedding.

It is important that everyone is ready to board the bus in Reykjavík at 10am on Friday April 17th. It is recommended that people arrive on Thursday and stay in or near Reykjavík.

If you are planning on leaving on the Sunday after the wedding, we won't be able to get you back to the airport for a morning flight. So please don't book don't book a flight intended to leave before midday.

FAQ

What should I wear...

  • To the tour: Be warm and comfortable. Dress is travel casual. You will be hopping on and off the bus to see what Iceland has to offer.
  • To the rehearsal dinner: Casual dinner dress.
  • To the ceremony: Feel free to get creative but we truly want you to be warm and comfortable. Ali will be in a wedding dress and likely boots, Josh will be in something a bit fancier than a black t-shirt. Please dress warm, if you would like to wear a dress or suit we will not stop you but please bring layers. The ceremony is going to be outside, some sort of outdoor shoes are advisable. There is a surprise outting after the ceremony that will be outdoors.
  • To the reception: Whatever wedding guest attire your heart desires. There will be drinks, food and dancing.

On plus ones

Please reach out to us if you had hoped to bring a guest. We have limited space but its very possible that we will have room. Please just ask us!

On kiddos

We love your kids very much but have chosen to make our wedding a 21 and over event. We are happy to assist in finding baby sitting services in Reykjavík if you would like to travel with your children.
